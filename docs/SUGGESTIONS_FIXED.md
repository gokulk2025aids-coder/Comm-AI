### For ANY Email Type:
1. **Analyzes Intent**: Request, Inquiry, Complaint, Follow-up, Meeting Request, Thank You, etc.
2. **Extracts Topics**: Identifies main subjects discussed
3. **Detects Sender Name**: From signature area (last 5 lines)
4. **Generates Custom Reply**: Based on intent + topics + tone
5. **Creates Improved Version**: Professional rewrite with proper structure

### Examples:

**Request Email** → Reply offers assistance with specific topic mentioned
**Complaint Email** → Reply apologizes and addresses the specific issue
**Meeting Request** → Reply suggests times and confirms availability
**Thank You Email** → Reply acknowledges and offers continued support
**Inquiry Email** → Reply provides information about the specific question
