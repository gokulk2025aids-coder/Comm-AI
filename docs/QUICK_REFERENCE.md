## 📊 Bulk Analysis - Quick Guide

### Add Emails:
- **Manual**: Click "Add Email" → Enter label → Paste content
- **File**: Click "Choose File" → Select .txt (emails separated by `---`)

### Analyze:
1. Click "Analyze All"
2. Wait for progress bar
3. View results automatically

### Chart Features:
- **4 bars per email**: Professionalism, Quality, Clarity, Engagement
- **Pagination**: Previous/Next for 10 emails per page
- **Hover**: See exact scores
- **Legend**: Click to hide/show metrics

### Export:
- **CSV**: Spreadsheet format
- **Excel**: Formatted table
- **Compare**: Side-by-side modal

---

## 📈 Reports - Quick Guide

### Generate Report:
1. Go to Reports (📈)
2. Choose Weekly (7 days) or Monthly (30 days)
3. Click "Generate Report"
4. Review all sections

### Report Sections:
- **Summary Stats**: Average scores
- **Improvements**: What got better
- **Needs Work**: What needs attention
- **Trends**: Patterns over time
- **Chart**: Visual progress
- **Insights**: AI recommendations

### Download PDF:
- Click "📄 Download PDF"
- Save for records
- Share with team
