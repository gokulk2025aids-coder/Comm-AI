### Problem:
- ❌ Clicking "View" button in bulk analysis showed browser alert
- ❌ Alert popup was not user-friendly
- ❌ Content was hard to read in alert box
- ❌ No way to copy content easily

### Solution:
- ✅ **Professional Modal Window** - Beautiful modal popup within the website
- ✅ **Better Formatting** - Proper text formatting with line breaks
- ✅ **Copy Button** - Easy one-click copy functionality
- ✅ **Scrollable Content** - Long emails can be scrolled
- ✅ **Clean UI** - Matches the app's design

## New Features:

### Modal Window Includes:
1. **Email Label** - Shows the email name/label
2. **Full Content** - Complete email text with proper formatting
3. **Copy Button** - Copy email content to clipboard
4. **Close Button** - Easy to close the modal

### Modal Design:
- Max width: 700px (comfortable reading)
- Max height: 400px for content (scrollable)
- Proper spacing and padding
- Theme-aware colors (works in light/dark mode)
- Professional appearance

## Usage:

1. Go to **Bulk Analysis** tab
2. Add emails (manually or upload file)
3. Click **"View"** button on any email
4. ✅ **Modal opens with full email content**
5. Read the email comfortably
6. Click **"Copy Content"** to copy
7. Click **"Close"** or X to close modal
