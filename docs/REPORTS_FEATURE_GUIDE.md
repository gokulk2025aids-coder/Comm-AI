# 📈 Weekly/Monthly Reports Feature

## Overview

The Reports feature provides comprehensive analysis summaries that track your email writing progress over time. Generate weekly or monthly reports to identify improvements, areas needing work, and writing trends.

---

## ✨ Features

### 1. **Report Types**

#### Weekly Report (Last 7 Days)
- Analyzes all emails from the past 7 days
- Perfect for tracking short-term progress
- Ideal for weekly team reviews
- Quick feedback on recent changes

#### Monthly Report (Last 30 Days)
- Comprehensive analysis of the past month
- Shows long-term trends and patterns
- Great for performance reviews
- Identifies sustained improvements

---

### 2. **Report Sections**

#### 📊 Summary Statistics
Displays average scores across all analyzed emails:
- **Total Emails Analyzed**: Count of emails in the period
- **Average Professionalism**: Score out of 10
- **Average Overall Quality**: Score out of 100
- **Average Readability**: How easy emails are to read
- **Average Clarity**: How clear messages are
- **Average Engagement**: How engaging content is
- **Average Grammar Issues**: Error count per email

**Color Coding:**
- 🟢 Green (80-100): Excellent
- 🔵 Blue (60-79): Good
- 🟡 Yellow (40-59): Fair
- 🔴 Red (0-39): Poor

---

#### 🌟 Most Improved Areas
Highlights areas where you've shown improvement:
- **Professionalism**: Writing quality improvements
- **Overall Quality**: General email quality gains
- **Grammar & Spelling**: Reduced error rates
- **Clarity**: Better message clarity
- **Engagement**: More engaging content

Each improvement shows:
- Area name
- Improvement amount (e.g., "+2.5 points")
- Description of the achievement

---

#### ⚠️ Areas Needing Work
Identifies areas requiring attention:
- **Professionalism**: Declining quality
- **Overall Quality**: Quality concerns
- **Grammar & Spelling**: High error rates
- **Clarity**: Unclear messaging
- **Engagement**: Low engagement scores

Each area shows:
- Issue description
- Current score or decline amount
- Actionable recommendations

---

#### 📈 Writing Trends
Tracks patterns in your email communication:
- **Professionalism Trend**: Improving/Declining/Stable
- **Overall Quality Trend**: Direction and change amount
- **Most Used Tone**: Your typical communication style
- **Most Common Intent**: Primary email purposes
- **Dominant Sentiment**: Overall emotional tone
- **Typical Priority**: Common urgency levels

**Trend Indicators:**
- 🟢 Improving: Positive change
- 🔴 Declining: Negative change
- 🔵 Stable: No significant change

---

#### 📉 Score Trends Over Time
Interactive line chart showing:
- Professionalism scores (×10 for scale)
- Overall quality scores
- Clarity scores
- Engagement scores

**Features:**
- Hover for exact values
- Click legend to hide/show metrics
- Visual trend identification
- Easy comparison across emails

---

#### 💡 Key Insights
AI-generated insights based on your data:
- Personalized recommendations
- Strengths to maintain
- Weaknesses to address
- Best practices reminders

**Example Insights:**
- "🌟 Excellent professional writing - keep it up!"
- "💡 Consider using more positive language"
- "📝 High grammar error rate - use spell-check"
- "😊 Great job maintaining positive communication"

## 🚀 How to Use

### Step 1: Navigate to Reports
1. Click **"Reports"** (📈) in the sidebar
2. You'll see two report type cards

### Step 2: Generate Report
1. Choose **Weekly Report** or **Monthly Report**
2. Click the **"Generate Report"** button
3. Wait for analysis (usually 1-2 seconds)

### Step 3: Review Results
1. Scroll through each section
2. Note your strengths and weaknesses
3. Review the trends chart
4. Read the key insights

### Step 4: Download PDF (Optional)
1. Click **"📄 Download PDF"** button
2. Save the PDF for your records
3. Share with team or manager if needed

## 📊 Understanding Your Scores

### Professionalism Score (0-10)
- **9-10**: Exceptional - Executive-level quality
- **7-8**: Good - Professional and polished
- **5-6**: Acceptable - Minor improvements needed
- **3-4**: Below Average - Significant revision required
- **1-2**: Poor - Complete rewrite needed

### Overall Quality Score (0-100)
- **90-100**: Outstanding - Best-in-class
- **80-89**: Excellent - Very high quality
- **70-79**: Good - Above average
- **60-69**: Acceptable - Room for improvement
- **50-59**: Below Average - Needs work
- **0-49**: Poor - Major improvements required

### Readability Score (0-100)
- **80+**: Very easy to read
- **60-79**: Moderately easy
- **40-59**: Somewhat difficult
- **0-39**: Very difficult

### Clarity Score (0-100)
- **80+**: Crystal clear
- **60-79**: Clear with minor ambiguities
- **40-59**: Somewhat unclear
- **0-39**: Very unclear

### Engagement Score (0-100)
- **80+**: Highly engaging
- **60-79**: Moderately engaging
- **40-59**: Somewhat engaging
- **0-39**: Not engaging

## 💡 Tips for Improvement

### If Professionalism is Low:
1. Use formal greetings and closings
2. Avoid casual language
3. Proofread before sending
4. Use professional vocabulary
5. Maintain respectful tone

### If Clarity is Low:
1. Use shorter sentences
2. Break into paragraphs
3. Use bullet points
4. Avoid jargon
5. Be direct and specific

### If Engagement is Low:
1. Ask questions
2. Use call-to-action
3. Add personal touches
4. Use positive language
5. Show enthusiasm

### If Grammar Issues are High:
1. Use spell-check tools
2. Read aloud before sending
3. Take your time
4. Use grammar checkers
5. Review common mistakes

## 🔧 Technical Details

### Data Requirements
- Minimum 1 email analyzed in period
- More emails = better insights
- Recommended: 5+ emails for trends

### Calculation Methods
- **Averages**: Mean of all scores
- **Trends**: First half vs second half comparison
- **Improvements**: Positive changes > 0.5 points
- **Needs Work**: Negative changes or low scores

### Privacy
- Reports are user-specific
- No data sharing between users
- Secure PDF generation
- Local storage only

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Why is my report empty?**
A: You need to analyze at least one email in the selected period (last 7 or 30 days).

**Q: How often should I generate reports?**
A: Weekly reports every Monday, monthly reports at month-end.

**Q: Can I compare reports?**
A: Yes! Download PDFs and compare manually, or track trends in the chart.

**Q: What if I have no improvements?**
A: Keep analyzing emails! Improvements show after consistent use.

**Q: Are reports saved?**
A: No, generate fresh reports each time. Download PDFs to save.

**Q: Can I share reports?**
A: Yes! Download PDF and share via email or file sharing.

**Q: How accurate are the insights?**
A: Based on actual analysis data - very accurate with 5+ emails.
